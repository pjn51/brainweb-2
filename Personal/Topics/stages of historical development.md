# Stages of Historical Development
---
The progress of humanity can be broadly broken down into a number of stages of developement, expressed as different modes of production. 

- [[primitive communism]]
- Barbarism
- [[the Asiatic mode of production]]
- [[slave society]]
- [[feudalism]]
- [[capitalism]]
- [[socialism]]
- [[communism]]
