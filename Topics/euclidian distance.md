# Euclidian Distance
`LINKS`: 
`TAGS`: #equation 

---
$$
\sqrt{ 
	(x_1 - y_1)^2 +
	(x_2 - y_2)^2 + 
	... +
	(x_n - y_n)^2
}
$$

- $x_n$ = observation *x* in dimension *n*
- $y_n$ = observation *y* in dimension *n*

Euclidian distance is a method for finding the distance between two points in *n* dimensions.
